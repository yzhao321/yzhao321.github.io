🌟 Biography

I am a first-year PhD student in the Department of Computer Science and Engineering at the University of California, Santa Cruz (UCSC), starting Summer 2023. I am very fortunate to be advised by Prof. Heiner Litz.

I obtained my BE degree from the Software Engineering Department at the South China University of Technology (SCUT) in 2020. I have also worked as a research assistant in the Chip Architecture Lab at SCUT and the Vehicle Security Lab at Zhejiang University (ZJU), under the supervision of Prof. Xiaohang Wang.

My research interests include computer architecture and operating systems.
