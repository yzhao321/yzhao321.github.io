🌟 Biography

I am a third-year PhD student in the Department of Computer Science and Engineering at the University of California, Santa Cruz (UCSC), starting in 2023. I am fortunate to be advised by Prof. Heiner Litz.

I obtained my BE degree from the Software Engineering Department at the South China University of Technology (SCUT) in 2020. I have worked as a research assistant in the Architecture Simulation Lab at SCUT and Zhejiang University (ZJU), under the supervision of Prof. Xiaohang Wang.

My research interests include computer architecture and operating systems.

💡 Projects

I am one of the authors of Scarab 2.0, a cycle-level CPU simulator.
